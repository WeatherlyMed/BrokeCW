Materials:
- 2x Flexy Bits (plastic from arizona gallon bottle)
- 2x 1/4" Coductive Pieces (Roofing Nails)
- Center Ground Rod (Random screw)
- Wire to connect everything (speaker wire)
- Insulative building materials (PVC and wood trim)


Place the center ground rod and a upright peice of building material on a flat peice of material

Attach the flex bits to the upright and add conductive peices at the other end
Wire it up and attach to a keyer
